Our Services

Evidence-based physiotherapy — what the science actually says.

🖐

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilisation, soft tissue massage, and manipulation. Works via neurophysiological mechanisms — not mechanical "realignment".

Neurophysiological Catalyst, not cure
🏃

Rehabilitation Exercises

Progressive exercise programs with proper overload. True rehab demands mechanical tension, metabolic stress, and progressive overload — not light resistance bands forever.

Progressive Overload Gold Standard
💊

Pain Management

Biopsychosocial approach — pain is a brain output, not just tissue damage. Graded exposure, sleep optimization, and stress management over passive rest.

Biopsychosocial Brain Output
🏥

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Criterion-based recovery — not time-based. Early mobilization, NMES to prevent atrophy, and objective testing before progressing to next phase.

Criterion-Based Not Time-Based
⚽

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

P.E.A.C.E. & L.O.V.E. protocol — R.I.C.E. is dead. Optimal loading, plyometrics, and reactive agility training to bridge clinical and sport demands.

PEACE & LOVE RICE is Dead
🧘

Posture Correction

Movement variability over rigid alignment. The best posture is your next posture. Build strength to tolerate different positions.

Movement Variability Myth Busted
⚡

Electrotherapy

TENS and targeted modalities used as temporary pain modulators — not primary treatment. Used to help irritable patients participate in active exercise.

TENS / Ultrasound Use Sparingly
🧍

Ergonomic Assessment

Micro-breaks, movement variability, and psychosocial stress management — more effective than a $1,000 ergonomic chair.

Workplace Health Move More
👴

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Heavy resistance training and reactive balance work. Older adults need to lift heavy — it's the only proven way to increase bone density and prevent falls.

Heavy Lifting No Ageism
