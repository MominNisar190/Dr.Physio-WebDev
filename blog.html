🖐 Manual Therapy

Benefits of Manual Therapy

Manual therapy works via neurophysiological mechanisms — not mechanical realignment.

Read More ↓

Touch stimulates mechanoreceptors, sending novel input to the CNS, which temporarily down-regulates pain perception and decreases muscle guarding.

💡 Manual therapy is a catalyst, not a cure. Use it to enable active movement.

📚 Mechanisms of Manual Therapy – Pain Medicine

🏃 Rehabilitation

How Rehabilitation Exercises Aid Recovery

The industry suffers from chronic under-dosing. Progressive overload is essential.

Read More ↓

True rehabilitation demands mechanical tension, metabolic stress, and progressive overload. Pain during rehab (3–4/10) is often necessary and safe.

💡 Transition from motor control exercises to heavy functional loading.

📚 Progression in Rehabilitation – BJSM

💊 Pain Management

Pain Management Strategies

Pain does not strictly equal tissue damage. The biomedical model is obsolete.

Read More ↓

Pain is influenced by the Biopsychosocial Model — biology, psychology, and sociology. Chronic pain is a sensitized nervous system misinterpreting safe signals.

💡 "Hurt does not equal harm." Graded exposure + sleep + stress management.

📚 The Lancet Series on Low Back Pain

⚽ Sports

Recovering from Sports Injuries

R.I.C.E. is dead. Even its creator has publicly retracted it.

Read More ↓

Ice suppresses macrophages, delaying tissue repair. The modern standard is P.E.A.C.E. & L.O.V.E. — Protect, Elevate, Avoid anti-inflammatories, Compress, Educate & Load, Optimism, Vascularization, Exercise.

💡 Optimal loading + plyometrics + reactive agility training.

📚 PEACE and LOVE – BJSM

🧘 Posture

The Posture Myth — Debunked

Almost zero correlation between spinal alignment and pain.

Read More ↓

The problem is not the shape of the posture, but the duration. Holding any posture for 8 hours causes ischemia and discomfort.

💡 The best posture is your next posture. Promote movement variability.

📚 Fall of the Postural Model – JBMT

🏥 Post-Surgery

Post-Surgical Rehab: What to Expect

Time-based healing is an average, not an absolute.

Read More ↓

Rehabilitation must be criterion-based. A patient earns the right to progress only when they pass specific objective tests — limb symmetry index, peak torque, psychological readiness.

💡 Early mobilization prevents arthrofibrosis. NMES prevents muscle atrophy.

📚 Criterion-Based Rehabilitation – JOSPT

⚡ Electrotherapy

The Truth About Electrotherapy

A large portion of electrotherapy is high-tech placebo.

Read More ↓

Therapeutic ultrasound is largely ineffective. TENS provides acute pain relief via Gate Control Theory but doesn't change tissue biology.

💡 Use only as a temporary pain modulator to enable active exercise.

📚 Effectiveness of TENS – Cochrane Library

🧍 Ergonomics

Ergonomics: What Actually Prevents Injury

Strict ergonomic interventions have largely failed to reduce injury rates.

Read More ↓

Disc herniations correlate more with genetics, poor sleep, and abrupt load spikes than with lifting mechanics. Movement breaks and cardiovascular fitness matter more.

💡 Micro-breaks + movement variability + psychosocial stress management.

📚 Workplace Ergonomic Interventions – Cochrane

👴 Geriatric Care

Why Seniors Need to Lift Heavy

The standard approach to geriatric physio is steeped in accidental ageism.

Read More ↓

Heavy resistance training is the only proven way to increase bone mineral density and stimulate fast-twitch muscle fibers — the exact fibers needed to catch yourself when you trip.

💡 Geriatric care should look like athletic performance training.

📚 Resistance Training for Older Adults – NSCA

🏠 Home Care

Why Home Physiotherapy Works

Better adherence, faster recovery, higher satisfaction than clinic visits.

Read More ↓

Home visits allow the physiotherapist to assess real-world functional challenges — stairs, furniture height, bathroom setup — and tailor the program accordingly.

💡 Environment-specific rehabilitation bridges clinical and real-world function.
